Place the provided Kitovu PNG here at: public/brands/kitovu.png

This repository currently references `/brands/kitovu.png` in `components/carousel-section.tsx`.

How to add the image locally (PowerShell):

  mkdir -Force public\brands;
  # Copy the actual PNG you attached into the folder, for example:
  # Copy-Item -Path C:\path\to\Kitovu.png -Destination .\public\brands\kitovu.png

Once the PNG is placed, the carousel will display it automatically.
